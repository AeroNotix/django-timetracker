Understanding your balance

Your balance is derived from taking your allocated shift length of {{ shiftlength }}, multiplying it by the amount of work days recorded in the database ({{ working_days }}) then taking the cumulative total of the time tracked and finding the difference between the projected total and the actual total.

Below is a mathematical representation:

      (shiftLength * (number_of_days_logged))
    - (total_time_logged)
